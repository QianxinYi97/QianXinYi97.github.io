About Me

I received the M.S. degree majoring in statistics from Xi'an Jiaotong University, Xi'an, China, in 2021. I am currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with the School of Management, advised by Associate Professor Yao Wang. My current research interests include tensor optimization algorithms and reinforcement learning.
